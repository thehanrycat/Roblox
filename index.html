About Ruckblox

Ruckblox is the no-limits, uncensored take on the classic Roblox experience. Run custom mods, bypass filters, unlock hidden features, join private servers, tweak physics, and play without corporate rules holding you back.

It's not official Roblox — it's the community rebellion. Perfect for veterans who want more freedom, custom GUIs, FPS unlocks, texture swaps, and pure chaos without getting patched every week.
